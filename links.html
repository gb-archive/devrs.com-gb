2000-03-05 - Paul Chinn's GB Page - Posted by: Jeff Frohwein
Paul Chinn disassembled the original arcade Qbert 8086 rom image and hand converted it into 'C' & z80 so he ended up with a port that plays identically to the arcade original. No sound support at this point. Any experienced sound developers willing to help out? This game is one of the few that will give the EXACT experience of the original arcade version!
